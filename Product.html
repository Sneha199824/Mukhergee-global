MAKHANA

Enjoy the savory crunch and nutritional advantages of our quality popped lotus seeds, Mukherjee Makhana. An ideal midday snack that is nutritious and can be substituted for traditional ones.

Makhana, also known as fox nuts, have many health benefits:

Heart health: Makhana contains potassium, which helps maintain heart muscle rhythm and condition. It also has anti-inflammatory properties that can lower the risk of heart disease. Makhana also contains sodium and magnesium, which can help with blood circulation, blood pressure, and overall heart function.

Digestive health: Makhana is high in fiber, which can improve digestion and prevent constipation.

Weight loss: Makhana is low in calories and cholesterol, and helps with weight loss.

Bone health: Makhana contains calcium, which can help strengthen bones and teeth.

Skin health: Makhana is rich in antioxidants, which can help with skin health and aging.

Hair health: Makhana can help with hair health.
